How to Use Our Database

1. Browsing Top and Current Airing Animes

  • Top 5 Airing Animes: On our homepage, you will find a section dedicated to the top 5 currently airing animes. This is a great place to discover the hottest shows that are currently trending.
  • All Airing Animes: We also feature a comprehensive list of all airing animes. Browse through this list to find new series to watch and enjoy.

2. Searching for Your Favorite Anime

Search Bar: At the top of our homepage, you'll find a search bar. Simply type in the name of the anime you're looking for, and our database will provide you with detailed information about it.

Anime Details: Click on any anime from the top 5 list, the current airing section, or the search results to access detailed information. You will be able to view the following details:
